Expectations

  • To be able to water referee with a high level referee
  • To practice referee skills safely
  • To identify fouls and start making calls in games
  • Must keep logbook of all games approved by higher level referees
  • Player Level Referees act mostly as an assistant to higher level referees in games, but are encouaged to learn and become more confident
PrerequisitesUSOA member, UWH club member, successful completion of level 1. At least 50 games reffed (20 WR, 15 DR, 3 T&S minimum) after level 1 course completion. A recommendation of 1 evaluator reviewing skills to the Level 1 Quality Standards. CertificationSuccessful completion of instructor led level 2 course, and passing of level 2 written test. Be a dedicated ref and be coached by an instructor for a USOA sanctioned tournament within a year. LimitationsLimitations Eligible to ref in any level of game in any referee position. Should only serve in positions to their level of competency.
