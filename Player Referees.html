Expectations

  • To be able to water referee with a high level referee
  • To practice referee skills safely
  • To identify fouls and start making calls in games
  • Must keep logbook of all games approved by higher level referees
  • Player Level Referees act mostly as an assistant to higher level referees in games, but are encouaged to learn and become more confident
Prerequisites: USOA member and/or UWH club member Certification: Complete online course Limitations Only referee round robin games.
