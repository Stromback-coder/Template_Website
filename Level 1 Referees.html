Expectations

  • To be able to referee in the water and condidently identify and make calls, be an arbitrator of disputes, and understand the rules of the game
  • To sharpen skills in identifying fouls and making calls
  • To encourage Player Referess to improve on their skills
  • Must keep logbook of all games refereed and approved by main active status
  • Be able to handle all referee roles in the tournament

Prerequisites:USOA member and/or UWH club member, Refereed at least 5 games, a recomendation of 1 evaluator
Certification:Complete instructer led level 1 course
Limitations:Only referee round robin and playoff games
