Level 2 Referees

Expectations:

  • Proficiency in advantage rule application
  • Demonstrates de-escalation techniques
  • Demonstrate critical thinking regarding penalty shot, penalty goal
  • Confidence in calls

Prerequisites:Level 1 referee with a recomondation and Pass level 2 class
Certification:Complete instructer led level 2 course
Limitations:Can referee any game no matter the level of play
